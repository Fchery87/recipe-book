Beverage Recipes

  • Rum Punch

    A tropical cocktail made with rum, fruit juices, and grenadine.

    Ingredients

    • Dark rum
    • Pineapple juice
    • Orange juice
    • Lime juice
    • Grenadine
    • Simple syrup
    • Angostura bitters

    Instructions

    Mix dark rum, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, grenadine, simple syrup, and a dash of angostura bitters. Serve over ice and garnish with a slice of lime or pineapple.
